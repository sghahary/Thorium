Business results

Our approach relentlessly centers on business results. We practically apply math and big data technology to achieve clear goals. We have delivered applications leveraging data to grow revenue through deeper insight into customer behaviors and segmentation; improve marketing through more effective resource allocation; and enhance sales force effectiveness by integrating real time analytics directly into sales activities.

Math

A key differentiator for Thorium is applying sophisticated quantitative techniques. We enhance business intelligence through advanced analytics, machine learning, and predictive models. We can apply these techniques for deeper insight, analyzing large data sets, and embedding real time insight within business processes.

Governance

Our team has extensive experience delivering large, complex, multi-stakeholder projects in global environments including within highly regulated business areas. We ensure clear goals, team alignment, transparency, and-most importantly-execution.

Vertical expertise

We bring industry understanding in consumer data and financial services based on extensive experience designing and delivering analytic, business intelligence, CRM, risk, and transactional systems.

Technical depth

Our culture emphasizes technical and quantitative depth. We are exceptionally rigorous in selecting people, and invest significantly to ensure team members' skills are at the forefront of innovation.

Design

Seeing value in large data sets benefits from elegant, user-centered design. Simplicity can be complex to achieve and maintain. We view graphic and interaction design as a core element to achieving business results.

Global

We have delivered large projects for complex organizations. Our team draws on broad experience adapting the core essentials of successful project delivery within local business cultures worldwide.

Big data

Our team applies expertise earned building and operating mission critical products based on Hadoop, HBase, and MongoDB. We bring practical, hands-on experience to accelerate and reduce risk to client results.

HP Vertica
