Who we are

Thorium started as a team of product designers, mathematicians, software engineers, and graphic designers with experience from the world's largest investment and commercial banks, digital marketing technology providers, and software companies. We have successfully delivered many large, complex, global applications and initiatives to achieve specific operating, financial, and strategic results.

Collectively, our experience includes:

  • Cross channel digital marketing
  • Consumer and business customer segmentation and targeting
  • Retail, capital markets, wealth management, retail banking, and telco
  • Post-merger integration bringing together data, applications, and processes into a single, coherent view
  • Due diligence on companies and technologies for investors

What we do

We design and build analytic and business intelligence systems to achieve targeted business results from data. We have built custom portals, sophisticated interactive business intelligence systems, sales workflow systems, risk analytics, and CRM systems. These applications apply quantitative techniques for aggregation, segmentation, prediction, and optimization. The models are based on a foundation of integrating and ensuring quality across a broad range of data sources.

How we work

Thorium takes a results-focused approach to deliver clear client impact. We fuse deep technical expertise with mathematic and quantitative techniques. We emphasize rigorous project governance to ensure clear goals, team alignment, and transparency to stakeholders. We work within an agile process to confirm direction early and bring business owners directly into projects. We emphasize elegant user interface design to effectively derive insights and actions from large data sets. Most importantly, we energize projects across disciplines and teams to execute.
