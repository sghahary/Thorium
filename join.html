Why work at Thorium?

We apply math to data for business results. We start with great people. We make an exceptional effort to identify and develop people who are among the best in their fields.

Thorium is differentiated by a distinct culture. We value results, intellect, and integrity. We encourage rapid, practical innovation. We put our people-centered culture in action by investing in developing people's skills and careers, open communication, and committing ourselves to positive and fair leadership.

We are based in New York, London, and Los Angeles with team members living and contributing from many locations.

  • Fun, intellectually challenging work environment.
  • Technology: Vertica, R, Python, Haskell, C++, Mahout, Java, SQL Server and SSAS, C#, Qlik, Hadoop, HBase, MongoDB
  • Rewarding utilization bonuses.
  • We value results, drive, intellect, communication and creativity more than credentials.
  • Global team and opportunities.
  • Flexibility to balance professional and personal commitments.
